The daily reality

Small tech problems have a big hidden cost.

A projector that won't connect. A login that won't work. A printer that's down. Each one pulls a teacher out of the lesson — and lands as one more ticket on an IT team that's already stretched thin.

For teachers

Lessons interrupted, momentum lost

Every minute spent troubleshooting a device is a minute taken from students. Teachers aren't IT staff — and they shouldn't have to be.

For IT teams

Buried in repetitive tickets

The same low-complexity issues, over and over, crowd out the project work that actually moves the institution forward.

How it works

Three simple steps. No tickets, no jargon.

A teacher reports an issue, EduDesk AI resolves it on the spot — and when it can't, a real person picks it up.

01

Report it

A teacher flags the problem in plain language — by app, chat or a quick message. Seconds, not a support form.

02

AI resolves it instantly

EduDesk AI knows your exact systems and fixes common issues on the spot — logins, projectors, printers, LMS access.

03

A human steps in when needed

Anything the AI can't solve goes straight to our remote support team. Either way, the teacher gets back to teaching.
